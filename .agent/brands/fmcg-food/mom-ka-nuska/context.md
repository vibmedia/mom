## Brand Purpose

**Why we exist:** Every Indian has tasted the magic of "Maa ke haath ka khana" — food made by a mother's hands with love, tradition, and care. That taste is disappearing from urban kitchens. Mom Ka Nuska exists to bring it back — not from a factory, but from a real mother, on a real farm, using real recipes passed through generations.

**What makes us different:** Anjula Singh — a real mother — is not just the inspiration, she IS the product. She creates, tests, and approves every recipe. The farm is real. The content is authentic. There is no brand spokesperson — the brand IS the person.

## Moat (Sustainable Competitive Advantage)

1. **Authenticity barrier** — Competitors can't replicate a real mother's story + real farm
2. **Content flywheel** — Daily farm content builds organic audience → reduces CAC over time
3. **Community** — "Maa ki community" of mothers sharing recipes → UGC + loyalty
4. **Regional depth** — State-wise recipe expertise is hard to replicate at scale
5. **Vertical integration** — Farm ownership controls quality + cost

## Target Audience

### Primary: Urban Millennials (25-40)

- Living in metros/Tier 1 cities
- Dual-income, time-poor, health-conscious
- Missing "ghar ka khana" taste
- Active on Instagram, YouTube
- Average monthly food spend: ₹3,000-8,000 on packaged/ordered food
- Value authenticity over price

### Secondary: NRI Families

- Living abroad, craving authentic Indian taste
- Willing to pay premium (2-3x domestic price)
- Emotion-driven purchase — nostalgia, festivals
- Order in bulk, gifting occasions

### Tertiary: Health-Conscious Families

- 35-55 age group, all tiers
- Concerned about preservatives, adulteration
- Looking for trusted "ghar jaisa" brands
- Price-sensitive but quality-first

## Website Strategy

### Location-First UX (Non-negotiable)

```
1. User lands on site
2. First prompt: "Where are you from?" (state/city selector or geolocation)
3. Product catalog filters to regional products first
4. National bestsellers shown below regional picks
5. NRI flow: auto-detect international IP → show NRI bundles + international shipping
```
