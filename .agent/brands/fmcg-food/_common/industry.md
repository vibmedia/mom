### Key Consumer Trends

1. **Health consciousness** — Clean label, no preservatives, organic preference
2. **Nostalgia buying** — Urban millennials seeking "ghar ka khana" taste
3. **Regional pride** — Demand for authentic state-specific recipes
4. **D2C trust** — Consumers increasingly buy directly from brands
5. **Content-driven purchase** — Food storytelling drives conversion
6. **Subscription models** — Monthly staple boxes gaining traction
7. **NRI segment** — High-value, emotion-driven repeat buyers
8. **Gifting** — Festival hampers, corporate gifts, wedding boxes

## Regulatory Framework

### FSSAI (Food Safety and Standards Authority of India)

| License Type | Who Needs It | Annual Turnover | Fee |
|-------------|-------------|----------------|-----|
| **Basic Registration** | Small food businesses | Up to ₹12 Lakh | ₹100/year |
| **State License** | Medium food businesses | ₹12 Lakh - ₹20 Crore | ₹2,000-5,000/year |
| **Central License** | Large / multi-state | Above ₹20 Crore | ₹7,500/year |

### Mandatory Label Requirements (FSSAI)

1. Product name
2. List of ingredients (descending order by weight)
3. Nutritional information (per 100g/100ml)
4. Net quantity
5. Name & address of manufacturer/marketer
6. FSSAI license number & logo
7. Batch/Lot number
8. Date of manufacture & best before date
9. Veg/Non-veg symbol (mandatory in India)
10. MRP (Maximum Retail Price)
11. Customer care details
12. Allergen declaration (if applicable)

### Other Compliance

| Requirement | Authority | When Needed |
|------------|----------|-------------|
| GST Registration | GST Council | Before first sale |
| Trademark | IP India (Controller General) | Brand name + logo (file early) |
| BIS Certification | Bureau of Indian Standards | For certain packaged food categories |
| Organic Certification | APEDA/NPOP | If claiming "organic" |
| AGMARK | Directorate of Marketing & Inspection | Optional, adds trust |
| Import-Export Code (IEC) | DGFT | When going international |

## Supply Chain & Operations

### Farm-to-Consumer Chain

```
Farm (raw materials)
  → Procurement & Quality Check
    → Processing Unit / Kitchen
      → Packaging
        → Warehousing
          → D2C: Direct ship via courier
          → B2B: Distributor → Retailer
          → Marketplace: Amazon/Flipkart/BigBasket fulfillment
```

### Key Cost Components

| Component | % of Revenue (typical) |
|-----------|----------------------|
| Raw materials | 25-35% |
| Processing & labor | 10-15% |
| Packaging | 8-12% |
| Logistics & shipping | 10-15% |
| Marketing & CAC | 15-25% |
| Platform commissions | 5-15% (marketplace) |
| Overheads | 5-10% |
| **Gross margin target** | **40-55%** |

### D2C Metrics Benchmarks (Indian Food Brands)

| Metric | Good | Great |
|--------|------|-------|
| CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost) | ₹200-400 | Below ₹150 |
| AOV (Average Order Value) | ₹400-600 | Above ₹800 |
| LTV (Lifetime Value) | ₹1,500-3,000 | Above ₹5,000 |
| Repeat Rate (6 months) | 20-30% | Above 40% |
| Gross Margin | 40-50% | Above 55% |
| Delivery TAT | 3-5 days | 1-2 days (metro) |

## Industry Terminology

| Term | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| **FMCG** | Fast-Moving Consumer Goods |
| **D2C / DTC** | Direct-to-Consumer |
| **F2C** | Farm-to-Consumer (our model) |
| **SKU** | Stock Keeping Unit (each unique product variant) |
| **MRP** | Maximum Retail Price |
| **MOQ** | Minimum Order Quantity |
| **HoReCa** | Hotels, Restaurants, Cafes (B2B channel) |
| **MT** | Modern Trade (supermarkets, hypermarkets) |
| **GT** | General Trade (kirana stores) |
| **PLR** | Product Listing Rate (for marketplaces) |
| **COGS** | Cost of Goods Sold |
| **BOM** | Bill of Materials (ingredient + packaging cost) |
| **Shelf Life** | Duration product remains safe and quality intact |
| **Clean Label** | Products with simple, recognizable ingredients |
