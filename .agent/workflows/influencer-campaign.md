description: Plan and execute an influencer marketing campaign for food products. From budget to ROI report.
---

## Steps

1. **Campaign Planning**
   - Define goal (awareness, sales, UGC)
   - Set budget (₹X for this campaign)
   - Choose product(s) to promote
   - Define success metrics (impressions, orders, ROI)

2. **Creator Shortlist**
   - Use `d2c-influencer-marketing` skill
   - Build list of 50-100 creators matching brand
   - Filter by: follower count, engagement rate, food niche, city
   - Categorize: barter vs paid
   - Output: Creator spreadsheet

3. **Outreach**
   - DM/email creators with personalized pitch
   - Negotiate terms (barter, paid, affiliate code)
   - Confirm 20-30 creators
   - Output: Confirmed creator list

4. **Brief & Ship**
   - Create campaign brief (what to show, what NOT to say)
   - Ship products with handwritten note from Mom
   - Include affiliate/coupon code
   - Output: Shipping tracking + brief sent

5. **Go Live & Monitor**
   - Track creator posts as they go live
   - Engage with every post (like, comment, share)
   - Repost best content to brand page
   - Monitor coupon code usage

6. **Amplify Top Performers**
   - Identify top 5 performing posts
   - Boost as paid ads (₹500-2K per post)
   - Negotiate ongoing partnership with top creators

7. **ROI Report**
   - Total reach and impressions
   - Orders attributed (by coupon code)
   - CAC from this campaign
   - Top 5 creators (for future campaigns)
   - Learnings and next campaign recommendations
   - Output: Campaign ROI Report
