---
description: Conduct comprehensive market research for a food product category or brand. TAM through positioning strategy.
---

## Steps

1. **Define Scope**
   - Which product category? (pickle, masala, RTC kit, etc.)
   - Which geography? (India national, state-specific, international)
   - Purpose? (investor prep, product launch, category entry)

2. **TAM/SAM/SOM**
   - Use `fmcg-market-intelligence` skill
   - Calculate TAM from industry reports (IBEF, Statista, IMARC)
   - Filter SAM by channel (D2C), geography, demographics
   - Estimate SOM based on marketing budget + production capacity
   - Output: Market sizing table with sources

3. **SWOT Analysis**
   - Internal: Strengths (recipe, story, team) + Weaknesses (scale, funding)
   - External: Opportunities (D2C growth, health trend) + Threats (FMCG giants, regulation)
   - Output: 2x2 SWOT matrix

4. **Competitor Deep-Dive**
   - Identify top 5-8 direct competitors + 3-5 indirect
   - Analyze: products, pricing, positioning, channels, content, reviews, funding
   - Find gaps and weaknesses to exploit
   - Output: Competitor analysis matrix

5. **Consumer Research**
   - Target audience demographics + psychographics
   - Buying behavior (where, when, why, how much)
   - Pain points with current options
   - Output: Customer persona cards

6. **Positioning Strategy**
   - Use `d2c-brand-building` skill
   - Define positioning statement
   - Differentiation matrix vs competitors
   - Output: Positioning document

7. **Final Report**
   - Compile all sections into Market Research Report
   - Executive summary with key findings and recommendations
   - Output: Complete Market Research Report
