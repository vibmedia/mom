description: Prepare for fundraising — financial model, pitch deck, data room, and investor outreach. Use before approaching investors.
---

## Steps

1. **Unit Economics**
   - Use `fmcg-funding-pitch` skill
   - Calculate COGS, gross margin, CAC, LTV, AOV
   - Validate LTV:CAC ratio >3:1
   - Output: Unit economics spreadsheet

2. **Financial Model**
   - 3-year revenue projection (monthly for Y1, quarterly for Y2-3)
   - Expense breakdown by category
   - Cash flow forecast
   - Three scenarios: pessimistic, realistic, optimistic
   - Output: Financial model (Excel/Sheets)

3. **Pitch Deck**
   - Use `fmcg-funding-pitch` skill (12-slide format)
   - Problem → Solution → Product → Market → Model → Traction → Competition → GTM → Team → Financials → Ask
   - Include product photos, farm photos, team photos
   - Output: Pitch deck (PDF/Slides)

4. **Data Room**
   - Incorporation documents
   - FSSAI license
   - Financial statements (if any)
   - Trademark filings
   - Product catalog with pricing
   - Team CVs
   - Market research summary
   - Customer testimonials (if any)
   - Output: Google Drive / Notion data room link

5. **Investor List**
   - Identify 20-30 relevant investors
   - Prioritize: FMCG-focused > consumer > generalist
   - Research each investor's portfolio, check size, thesis
   - Find warm intros where possible
   - Output: Investor CRM spreadsheet

6. **Practice Pitch**
   - Rehearse 12-slide deck: target 12 minutes + 8 min Q&A
   - Prepare answers for top 20 investor questions
   - Practice with mentors / advisors first
   - Gate: Can deliver deck smoothly in 12 minutes

7. **Outreach & Follow-up**
   - Send warm intro emails (preferred) or cold pitch
   - Follow up at Day 3, Day 7, Day 14
   - Track responses in investor CRM
   - Schedule meetings
   - Output: Meeting schedule + follow-up tracker
