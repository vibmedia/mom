# FMCG Pricing & Offers

> Price to position, bundle to upsell, offer to convert.

## Core Framework

### Pricing Psychology for Food

| Principle | Application |
|-----------|------------|
| **Charm pricing** | ₹199 > ₹200 (always end in 9) |
| **Anchor high** | Show ₹499 jar next to ₹249 jar (makes ₹249 feel like a deal) |
| **Bundle value** | "3 for ₹499" (each ₹166 vs individual ₹199) |
| **Subscription discount** | "Subscribe & save 15%" — locks in repeat |
| **Free shipping threshold** | "Free shipping on orders above ₹499" — increases AOV |
| **Limited edition** | "Festival special — only 500 jars" — urgency + premium pricing |

### Offer Types for Food D2C

| Offer | When | Impact | Example |
|-------|------|--------|---------|
| **First-order discount** | Acquisition | High conversion | "Use FIRST20 for 20% off" |
| **BOGO** | Clearance, trial | Moves volume | "Buy 1 Achar, Get 1 Chutney Free" |
| **Combo Pack** | Upsell | Increases AOV | "Kitchen Essentials: 5 products for ₹799" |
| **Festival Hamper** | Seasonal | Premium pricing | "Diwali Gift Box — ₹1,499" |
| **Subscription** | Retention | Locks in LTV | "Monthly Masala Box — ₹399/month (save 15%)" |
| **Referral** | Viral growth | Low CAC | "Give ₹100, Get ₹100" |
| **Flash Sale** | Urgency | Spike in orders | "24-hour sale: 30% off all pickles" |
| **Sample Kit** | Trial | Low barrier | "Taste 5 flavors for ₹149" |
| **Free product** | Surprise delight | Repeat rate | "Free chutney with every order above ₹500" |

### Upselling & Cross-Selling Matrix

| If Customer Buys | Upsell To | Cross-Sell |
|-----------------|-----------|-----------|
| Single jar pickle | Family pack (save 20%) | Papad + Chutney combo |
| Masala mix | 3-masala bundle | Ready-to-cook kit |
| Namkeen | Party pack size | Festival hamper |
| Any product | Subscription (save 15%) | Sample kit of other categories |

## Common Mistakes

- ❌ Running discounts too often (trains customers to wait for sales)
- ❌ Discounting below margin (loss leaders need a profitable upsell path)
- ❌ Not tracking offer performance by code
- ❌ Same offers for all customer segments (new vs returning need different treatment)
