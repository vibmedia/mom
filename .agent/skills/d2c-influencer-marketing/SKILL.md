# D2C Influencer Marketing

> Low-cost, high-impact influencer strategies for food brands. Barter before budget.

## Core Framework

### Influencer Tiers for Food Brands

| Tier | Followers | Cost | Best For | Expected ROI |
|------|----------|------|----------|-------------|
| **Nano** | 1-5K | Free (barter) | UGC, testimonials | High engagement |
| **Micro** | 5-25K | ₹500-2K + product | Local reach, trust | Best ROI |
| **Mid** | 25-100K | ₹3-10K + product | Category awareness | Good ROI |
| **Macro** | 100K-1M | ₹15-50K | Brand launch spike | Lower ROI |
| **Mega** | 1M+ | ₹1-10L | Mass awareness | Variable |

> **Recommendation:** Focus 80% of budget on Nano + Micro. They convert better for food.

### Barter Deal Framework

| Step | Action |
|------|--------|
| 1 | Build list of 100 food creators (Instagram + YouTube) in target cities |
| 2 | DM: "Hi [name], we love your content! We're a homemade food brand — would you try our [product] and share honest thoughts?" |
| 3 | Ship product with handwritten note from Mom (Anjula Singh) |
| 4 | Follow up gently after 5-7 days |
| 5 | Repurpose their content (with permission) for ads and social |

### Affiliate Program Structure

| Element | Setup |
|---------|-------|
| **Commission** | 10-15% of sale value |
| **Cookie duration** | 30 days |
| **Payment** | Monthly, min ₹500 payout |
| **Tracking** | Unique coupon code per creator (e.g., FOODIE10) |
| **Tools** | GoAffPro (Shopify), Refersion, or manual tracking |
| **Bonus tiers** | 10 sales = extra product; 50 sales = double commission |

### Campaign Execution Playbook

| Phase | Duration | Activities |
|-------|----------|-----------|
| **Plan** | Week 1 | Set goal, budget, creator list, brief |
| **Outreach** | Week 2 | DM/email creators, negotiate, confirm |
| **Ship** | Week 3 | Send products, include brief + note |
| **Go Live** | Week 4-5 | Creators post, monitor, engage |
| **Amplify** | Week 5-6 | Boost top-performing creator content as ads |
| **Report** | Week 6 | Track conversions, ROI, learnings |

### Campaign Brief Template

```markdown
# Influencer Brief — [Campaign Name]

## Product
- Name: [product]
- Key claims: [zero preservatives, homemade, etc.]
- Price: [MRP]

## What We Need
- 1 Instagram Reel (30-60 sec) OR 1 YouTube Short
- Honest review/taste test (not scripted)
- Tag @momkanuska + use #MomKaNuska

## What To Highlight
- Taste reaction (genuine!)
- Packaging quality
- "Made by a real mom on a real farm"

## What NOT To Say
- Don't call it "factory-made" or "industrial"
- Don't make health claims we haven't verified
- Don't compare directly to competitors by name

## Compensation
- [ ] Barter (free product only)
- [ ] ₹[amount] + product
- [ ] Affiliate code: [CODE] (10% commission)
```

## Common Mistakes

- ❌ Scripting exact words for creators (kills authenticity)
- ❌ Only working with big creators (micro converts better for food)
- ❌ Not tracking conversions per creator
- ❌ Sending product without a brief
- ❌ Ignoring regional food creators (they have hyper-loyal audiences)
