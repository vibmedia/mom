# FMCG Legal & Compliance

> Navigate incorporation, FSSAI, GST, trademarks, and legal milestones for Indian food brands.

## When to Use

- Starting a new food business (what to register first?)
- Choosing incorporation type (Pvt Ltd vs LLP vs OPC)
- Getting FSSAI license
- Understanding GST for food products
- Filing trademarks for brand protection
- Planning legal milestones timeline
- Understanding accounting/CA requirements for each role

## Before Starting

1. **What stage is the business?** Idea, pre-revenue, revenue-generating?
2. **How many founders?** Single, 2, or more?
3. **Planning to raise external funding?** (affects incorporation choice)
4. **Which states will you sell in?** (affects FSSAI license type)

## Core Framework

### Incorporation Types (India)

| Type | Best For | Min Directors | Compliance | Fundraising | Cost |
|------|----------|--------------|------------|-------------|------|
| **Pvt Ltd (Recommended)** | Planning to raise funding | 2 | High (annual filings, audit) | ✅ Best for investors | ₹8-15K |
| **LLP** | Bootstrapped, 2+ founders | 2 (partners) | Medium | ⚠️ Hard to raise equity | ₹5-10K |
| **OPC (One Person Co.)** | Solo founder, early stage | 1 | Medium | ⚠️ Limited, converts to Pvt Ltd | ₹6-12K |
| **Sole Proprietorship** | Testing, micro-scale | 1 | Low | ❌ No equity possible | ₹1-3K |
| **Partnership Firm** | Family business, simple | 2+ | Low | ❌ No equity possible | ₹2-5K |

> **Recommendation for F2C:** Start as **Pvt Ltd** from Day 1 — you plan to raise capital.

### Incorporation Checklist

| Step | What | Timeline | Cost |
|------|------|----------|------|
| 1 | Obtain DSC (Digital Signature Certificate) | 1-2 days | ₹500-1,500 |
| 2 | Apply for DIN (Director Identification Number) | 1-2 days | Included in SPICe+ |
| 3 | Reserve company name (RUN form / SPICe+ Part A) | 1-3 days | ₹1,000 |
| 4 | File SPICe+ (incorporation + PAN + TAN + GST) | 3-7 days | ₹3,000-5,000 |
| 5 | Open current bank account | 1-7 days | Free |
| 6 | Issue share certificates | Same day | Internal |
| 7 | Register on MCA portal, file INC-20A | Within 180 days | ₹500 |

### FSSAI Licensing Roadmap

| License | When | Turnover | Process |
|---------|------|----------|---------|
| **Basic Registration** | Day 1 (start selling) | Up to ₹12L/year | Online at foscos.fssai.gov.in, 7-14 days |
| **State License** | When crossing ₹12L OR multi-district | ₹12L - ₹20Cr | Apply online, inspection, 30-60 days |
| **Central License** | Multi-state OR ₹20Cr+ | Above ₹20Cr | Apply online, inspection, 60-90 days |

**Documents needed:**
- Photo ID (Aadhar/PAN)
- Address proof of premises
- Incorporation certificate (if company)
- Food safety management plan (for State/Central)
- List of food products
- Water test report (for manufacturing)
- Blueprint of processing unit

### GST Registration & Compliance

| Task | When | How |
|------|------|-----|
| GST Registration | Before first sale (or when turnover crosses ₹20L for services, ₹40L for goods) | Online at gst.gov.in |
| Monthly GSTR-3B | By 20th of every month | GST portal / CA |
| Quarterly GSTR-1 | If under ₹5Cr turnover | GST portal / CA |
| Annual GSTR-9 | By Dec 31 of next FY | GST portal / CA |

### Trademark Protection

| Step | Timeline | Cost | Notes |
|------|----------|------|-------|
| TM search (check availability) | 1 day | Free on ipindia.gov.in | Search BEFORE finalizing brand name |
| File TM application | 1-2 days | ₹4,500 (startup) / ₹9,000 (company) | File in Class 29 (food) and Class 30 (spices) |
| TM published in journal | 3-6 months | — | Others can oppose |
| TM registered (if no opposition) | 8-12 months | — | Valid for 10 years, renewable |
| Logo TM (separate) | Same process | Same cost | File separately for logo |

> **Tip:** File TM in Class 29 (preserved foods) AND Class 30 (spices, condiments) for full protection.

### Accounting Knowledge by Role

| Role | Must Know | Can Delegate |
|------|----------|-------------|
| **Founder/CEO** | P&L reading, cash flow management, burn rate, unit economics, cap table, GST basics, MIS reports | Bookkeeping, tax filing, compliance |
| **Investor** | Cap table, valuation, dilution, SAFE/convertible notes, shareholder agreement, DRHP basics | Day-to-day accounting |
| **CPO** | COGS, BOM (Bill of Materials), margin per SKU, raw material pricing, inventory valuation | Invoice generation, GST compliance |
| **CTO** | Tech spend vs budget, SaaS costs, hosting costs, payment gateway fees | All other accounting |
| **Legal Team** | Compliance calendar, FSSAI renewal dates, trademark surveillance, MCA filings, board resolutions | Financial statements |

### CA (Chartered Accountant) Requirements

| Task | Frequency | Estimated Cost |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| Monthly bookkeeping | Monthly | ₹2,000-5,000/month |
| GST filing | Monthly/Quarterly | ₹1,000-3,000/filing |
| TDS compliance | Monthly (if applicable) | ₹500-1,000/month |
| Income tax return | Annual | ₹5,000-15,000 |
| Statutory audit | Annual (Pvt Ltd mandatory) | ₹15,000-50,000 |
| ROC annual filings | Annual | ₹5,000-10,000 |

### Legal Milestones Timeline

| When | Milestone | Mandatory? |
|------|-----------|-----------|
| **Month 0** | Decide incorporation type | ✅ |
| **Month 0-1** | Incorporate company (Pvt Ltd) | ✅ |
| **Month 0-1** | Open current bank account | ✅ |
| **Month 0-1** | File trademark application | ✅ (protect brand early) |
| **Month 0-2** | FSSAI Basic Registration | ✅ (before selling) |
| **Month 0-2** | GST Registration | ✅ (before selling) |
| **Month 0-2** | MSME Registration (Udyam) | Recommended (for loans) |
| **Month 0-3** | Founders' agreement + SHA | ✅ (if multiple founders) |
| **Month 3-6** | Professional liability insurance | Recommended |
| **Month 6-12** | Upgrade to FSSAI State License | When turnover crosses ₹12L |
| **Year 1** | First statutory audit + ROC filing | ✅ |
| **Year 1-2** | FSSAI renewal | ✅ (annual) |
| **Year 2-3** | ISO 22000 (food safety) | Optional but adds trust |
| **Year 3+** | International expansion: IEC, APEDA registration | When ready |
| **Year 5+** | Consider FSSAI Central License | If multi-state scale |

### International Expansion Readiness

| Requirement | What | When |
|-------------|------|------|
| IEC (Import Export Code) | Register with DGFT | Before first export order |
| APEDA Registration | For processed food exports | Before exporting |
| FDA Registration (USA) | If selling in USA | Before shipping to USA |
| Country-specific labeling | Varies (UK FSA, EU regulations) | Per destination country |
| Halal/Kosher certification | For Middle East / Jewish markets | If targeting those markets |

## Common Mistakes

- ❌ Starting as sole proprietorship when planning to raise funding
- ❌ Selling without FSSAI — heavy penalties (up to ₹5L)
- ❌ Not filing GST from Month 1 — kills bank loan eligibility
- ❌ Delaying trademark — someone else files your brand name
- ❌ Not having a founders' agreement — leads to disputes
- ❌ Mixing personal and business bank accounts
