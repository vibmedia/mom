# FMCG Product Development

> Standardize recipes, intake new products, and maintain authenticity across scale.

## When to Use

- Developing a new food product from recipe to shelf-ready
- Standardizing a home recipe for production
- Adapting recipes for different regional palates
- Shelf-life testing and quality assurance
- Creating product intake SOPs

## Core Framework

### New Product Intake Pipeline

| Stage | Activities | Gate |
|-------|-----------|------|
| 1. **Idea** | Market need, category fit, team capability | CPO approves concept |
| 2. **Recipe** | Kitchen development, 3+ iterations | CPO taste test pass |
| 3. **Standardize** | Measure all ingredients, document process | Written SOP complete |
| 4. **Test** | 5 batches for consistency, shelf-life test | Quality pass |
| 5. **Cost** | BOM calculation, margin check | >45% gross margin |
| 6. **Comply** | FSSAI category, label requirements, nutrition | FSSAI-ready |
| 7. **Package** | Design, material, size, seal test | Package prototype approved |
| 8. **Photograph** | Product + lifestyle shots for listing | Content ready |
| 9. **Launch** | List on D2C + marketplace, price, activate | Live |

```markdown
### Quality Gate

```

### Regional Palate Matrix

| Parameter | North | South | West | East | Central |
|-----------|-------|-------|------|------|---------|
| **Spice level** | High | Medium-High | Medium | Low-Medium | Medium-High |
| **Sweetness** | Medium | Low | High (Gujarat) | Medium | Low |
| **Oil type** | Mustard/Ghee | Coconut/Sesame | Peanut/Sunflower | Mustard | Soybean/Mustard |
| **Base flavor** | Garam masala | Curry leaf, coconut | Kokum, peanut | Panch phoron | Forest spices |
| **Heat source** | Red chilli | Green chilli, pepper | Kokum, tamarind | Mustard | Red chilli |
| **Signature** | Rich, layered | Tangy, aromatic | Sweet-savory | Subtle, mustard | Earthy, warming |

### Shelf Life Testing Protocol

| Test | Method | Frequency |
|------|--------|-----------|
| **Accelerated** | Store at 37°C/75% RH, check weekly | Before first launch |
| **Real-time** | Store at ambient, check monthly | Ongoing for each SKU |
| **Parameters** | Color, aroma, taste, texture, pH, moisture | At each check |
| **Microbiology** | Send to NABL-accredited lab | Quarterly |

## Common Mistakes

- ❌ Relying on memory instead of written SOPs
- ❌ Skipping shelf-life testing ("it tastes fine to me")
- ❌ Not calculating BOM before pricing
- ❌ Making all products equally spicy (India's palate varies wildly)
- ❌ Over-engineering for scale before validating demand
