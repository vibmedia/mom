# FMCG B2B Integration

> Expand revenue through B2B channels without diluting the D2C brand.

## Core Framework

### B2B Channel Options

| Channel | Margin | Volume | Brand Control | Start When |
|---------|--------|--------|--------------|-----------|
| **Amazon/Flipkart** | Low (30-40% after fees) | High | Medium | Month 6+ |
| **BigBasket/Blinkit** | Low (35-45% after fees) | Medium-High | Low | Month 12+ |
| **HoReCa (Hotels, Restaurants, Cafes)** | Medium (40-50%) | Medium | Low (bulk/unbranded) | Month 12+ |
| **Modern Trade (D-Mart, More)** | Low (25-35%) | High | Medium | Month 18+ |
| **Distributor/Stockist** | Very Low (20-30%) | Very High | Low | Month 24+ |
| **White-Label** | Medium (30-40%) | High | None | When capacity allows |
| **Co-branding** | Variable | Medium | Shared | Strategic partnerships |
| **Corporate Gifting** | High (50-60%) | Seasonal spike | High | Month 6+ |

### White-Label Model

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **What** | Produce food under another brand's name |
| **Why** | Revenue without marketing cost, utilize excess capacity |
| **Risk** | Competes with own brand at different price point |
| **Protect** | Never white-label your hero product. Only produce commodities or different recipes |
| **Pricing** | COGS + 20-30% margin minimum |
| **Contracts** | MOQ, quality standards, payment terms, exclusivity clause |

### HoReCa Approach

| Step | Action |
|------|--------|
| 1 | Identify 50 local restaurants, cafes, and hotels |
| 2 | Offer free sample kit with price list |
| 3 | Start with 2-3 products (pickles, chutneys work best) |
| 4 | Bulk packaging (1kg, 5kg options) |
| 5 | Monthly delivery schedule |
| 6 | Invoice: 15-30 day payment terms |

### Marketplace Selling (Amazon/Flipkart)

| Requirement | Details |
|-------------|---------|
| **GST Registration** | Mandatory |
| **FSSAI license** | Mandatory (number on listing) |
| **Product photos** | White background + lifestyle (min 5) |
| **A+ Content** | Brand story, usage instructions, ingredients |
| **FBA vs Self-ship** | FBA recommended for Prime badge, faster delivery |
| **Pricing** | Account for 30-40% commission + shipping |
| **Reviews** | First 25 reviews are critical — seed with early customers |

### B2B Pricing Strategy

| Channel | Your Cost | Your Price | Their Markup | End Consumer |
|---------|-----------|-----------|-------------|-------------|
| **D2C** | ₹60 | ₹199 | — | ₹199 |
| **Amazon** | ₹60 | ₹199 MRP | 30% commission | ₹199 (you get ~₹139) |
| **HoReCa** | ₹60 | ₹100-120 | Their menu price | Varies |
| **Distributor** | ₹60 | ₹90-100 | 30-40% | ₹140-160 |
| **White-label** | ₹60 | ₹75-85 | Their brand pricing | Varies |

## Common Mistakes

- ❌ Entering B2B before D2C brand is established (dilutes focus)
- ❌ Pricing B2B the same as D2C (channel economics are different)
- ❌ White-labeling your hero product (cannibalizes your brand)
- ❌ Ignoring corporate gifting (low effort, high margin, seasonal spike)
- ❌ Not having separate GST invoicing for B2B vs B2C
