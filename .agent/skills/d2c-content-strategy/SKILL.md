# D2C Content Strategy

> Content-first growth for food brands. Farm is the studio, Mom is the star.

## Core Framework

### Content Pillars (Food D2C)

| Pillar | Purpose | Format | Frequency |
|--------|---------|--------|-----------|
| **Farm Diary** | Build daily connection, authenticity | Reels/Shorts (15-30s) | Daily |
| **Recipe Heritage** | Showcase product + culture + story | YouTube (5-10 min) | 2x/week |
| **Behind the Pack** | Transparency, trust building | Reels/Stories | 1x/week |
| **Regional Spotlight** | Celebrate India's food diversity | Carousel/Video | 1x/week |
| **Customer Love** | Social proof, UGC | Stories/Posts | Ongoing |
| **Festival Specials** | Seasonal relevance, gifting push | All formats | Seasonal |

### YouTube Channel Strategy

| Content Type | Length | Goal | Upload |
|-------------|--------|------|--------|
| **Recipe videos** | 5-10 min | Search traffic, long-tail | 2x/week |
| **Behind-the-scenes** | 3-5 min | Trust, engagement | 1x/week |
| **YouTube Shorts** | 15-60 sec | Discovery, viral reach | Daily |
| **Monthly vlogs** | 10-15 min | Deep brand story | 1x/month |

### UGC Campaign Framework

| Campaign | Mechanic | Incentive | Platform |
|----------|----------|-----------|----------|
| **#MomKaNuska Challenge** | "Show us YOUR mom's recipe" | Feature on page + free product | Instagram |
| **Unboxing Club** | Customers film unboxing | Discount on next order | Instagram/YouTube |
| **Recipe Remix** | "Use our masala in YOUR dish" | Weekly winner gets hamper | Instagram/YouTube |
| **Review Rewards** | Written review on site | ₹50 store credit | Website |

### YouTube Decode: What Works for Food Brands

| Pattern | Why It Works | Our Version |
|---------|-------------|-------------|
| **ASMR cooking** | Satisfying visuals, no talking needed | Mom cooking with natural sounds |
| **"I tried making [X]"** | Curiosity, relatable | "Mom tries making [trending recipe]" |
| **Taste test/reaction** | Authentic emotion | Customer first-bite reactions |
| **"How [X] is really made"** | Transparency sells | Farm-to-pack journey |
| **Regional food travel** | Discovery, nostalgia | "Rajasthani pickles of my childhood" |

## Common Mistakes

- ❌ Treating all platforms the same (each has its own algorithm and audience)
- ❌ Over-producing content (raw, authentic farm content > studio content)
- ❌ Posting without a content pillar (random posts don't build brand)
- ❌ Ignoring Shorts/Reels (they're the #1 discovery channel in 2025+)
- ❌ Not repurposing (1 recipe video = 5 Reels + 3 Stories + 1 blog post)
