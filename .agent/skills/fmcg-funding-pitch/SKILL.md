# FMCG Funding & Pitch

> Prepare compelling pitches, build financial models, and navigate fundraising for food brands.

## When to Use

- Preparing a seed funding pitch deck
- Building financial models (unit economics, projections)
- Understanding how much to raise and at what valuation
- Exploring bank financing (MSME, Mudra, CGTMSE loans)
- Planning the fundraising timeline (Angel → Seed → Series A)
- Understanding what investors look for in FMCG D2C

## Before Starting

1. **What stage are you at?** Pre-product, pre-revenue, post-revenue?
2. **How much do you need?** And for what specific milestones?
3. **What's your current burn rate?** Monthly expenses?
4. **Do you have any traction?** Sales, waitlist, social following?

## Core Framework

### How Much Seed Funding to Raise

| Category | Estimated Cost | Notes |
|----------|---------------|-------|
| Farm land (lease 1 year) | ₹3-8 Lakh | Depends on location/state |
| Kitchen infrastructure | ₹5-10 Lakh | Processing unit + equipment |
| Content studio setup | ₹2-5 Lakh | Camera, lighting, space |
| FSSAI + legal + incorporation | ₹1-2 Lakh | Registration + trademark |
| Initial raw materials | ₹2-4 Lakh | First 3-month supply |
| Packaging (first batch) | ₹3-5 Lakh | Design + printing + materials |
| D2C website + tech | ₹1-3 Lakh | Shopify or custom |
| Marketing (launch) | ₹3-5 Lakh | Influencer + ads + content |
| Working capital buffer | ₹5-8 Lakh | 3-month runway |
| **Total seed requirement** | **₹25-50 Lakh** | **12-18 month runway** |

### Investor Pitch Deck (12-Slide Format)

| Slide | Content | Time |
|-------|---------|------|
| 1. **Cover** | Brand name, tagline, one-line description | 10 sec |
| 2. **Problem** | Urban Indians miss ghar ka khana. Packaged food is preservative-laden | 1 min |
| 3. **Solution** | Real mother, real farm, real recipes — delivered to your door | 1 min |
| 4. **Product** | Hero products with photos, pricing, packaging | 1 min |
| 5. **Market** | TAM/SAM/SOM with credible sources | 1 min |
| 6. **Business Model** | D2C + Marketplace + B2B. Unit economics | 1.5 min |
| 7. **Traction** | Any early signal: waitlist, social, pilot orders | 1 min |
| 8. **Competition** | Positioning matrix — how you're different | 1 min |
| 9. **Go-to-Market** | Content-first strategy, influencer seeding, launch plan | 1 min |
| 10. **Team** | Founder (strategy/tech), Mother (CPO/product), Sister (branding) | 1 min |
| 11. **Financials** | 3-year projection, key metrics, path to profitability | 1.5 min |
| 12. **Ask** | ₹25-50L for 18 months runway, milestones, use of funds | 1 min |

### Unit Economics Template

| Metric | Formula | Target |
|--------|---------|--------|
| **COGS per unit** | Raw material + processing + packaging | ₹40-80 |
| **MRP** | Market-appropriate price | ₹149-299 |
| **Selling price (net)** | After platform commission, GST | ₹100-200 |
| **Gross margin** | (Selling price - COGS) / Selling price | 50-60% |
| **CAC** | Total marketing spend / New customers | ₹150-300 |
| **AOV** | Average order value | ₹400-600 |
| **LTV** | AOV × Purchase frequency × Lifespan | ₹1,500-3,000 |
| **LTV:CAC ratio** | LTV / CAC | >3:1 |
| **Payback period** | Months to recover CAC | <3 months |
| **Contribution margin** | Gross profit - delivery - payment processing | 30-40% |

### Bank Financing Strategy

| Loan Type | Amount | Rate | Who Qualifies |
|-----------|--------|------|--------------|
| **Mudra Shishu** | Up to ₹50,000 | 7-10% | Any startup, no collateral |
| **Mudra Kishore** | ₹50K - ₹5L | 8-12% | With some revenue |
| **Mudra Tarun** | ₹5L - ₹10L | 10-14% | Established small business |
| **CGTMSE** | Up to ₹5 Cr | 8-12% | Collateral-free, government guarantee |
| **MSME Term Loan** | ₹10L - ₹2 Cr | 10-14% | Registered MSME with 1+ year |
| **Cash Credit/OD** | Based on turnover | 10-16% | For working capital, needs turnover history |

### Making Your Brand "Bankable"

Banks want to see:

| Factor | How to Build It |
|--------|----------------|
| **GST filing history** | File from Day 1, even if zero returns |
| **Regular revenue** | Even ₹10K/month, show consistent growth |
| **Current account activity** | Use business account for ALL transactions |
| **FSSAI license** | Shows regulatory compliance |
| **Clean books** | Monthly CA-reviewed balance sheets |
| **Business plan** | Written, with projections (not just verbal) |
| **Collateral** | Property, FD, or CGTMSE guarantee |

### Cash Flow Strategy: Local Product Placement

| Tactic | How It Works | Cash Flow Impact |
|--------|-------------|-----------------|
| **Consignment in local stores** | Place products in kiranas, bill on sale | Slow but builds proof |
| **Bulk pre-orders** | Collect advances for festival packs | Immediate cash inflow |
| **Subscription model** | Monthly/quarterly auto-delivery | Predictable revenue |
| **Corporate gifting** | B2B festival hampers | High-margin, upfront payment |
| **Popup stalls** | Weekend markets, festivals, exhibitions | Cash-on-sale + brand visibility |

## Common Mistakes

- ❌ Asking for too much without milestones ("give me ₹1 Cr, trust me")
- ❌ No unit economics — investors need to see path to margin
- ❌ Comparing yourself to Haldiram's — you're not competing with them yet
- ❌ Ignoring bank financing — it's non-dilutive and available
- ❌ Not filing GST from Day 1 — kills your bank loan chances later
- ❌ Spending all funding on marketing with no product-market fit proof
