# FMCG Farm Setup

> Plan and build a small farm that serves triple duty: ingredient sourcing, product testing, and authentic content creation.

## When to Use

- Searching for farm land
- Planning farm infrastructure
- Designing a kitchen/processing unit
- Setting up a content studio
- Planning farm operations and staffing
- Estimating farm setup budget

## Before Starting

1. **Which state/region?** Climate, land prices, and crops vary dramatically
2. **Budget?** How much is allocated for farm setup?
3. **Primary purpose?** Content + testing + production? Or content + testing only?
4. **Scale?** How many SKUs and what volume initially?

## Core Framework

### Farm Land Selection Criteria

| Factor | Ideal | Acceptable | Avoid |
|--------|-------|-----------|-------|
| **Distance from city** | 30-60 km | 60-100 km | >100 km (logistics nightmare) |
| **Road access** | Paved road to gate | 500m from paved road | Unpaved, seasonal access |
| **Water source** | Borewell + municipal | Borewell only | No reliable water |
| **Electricity** | 3-phase power | Single phase + inverter/solar | No grid connection |
| **Land size** | 1-2 acres | 0.5-1 acre | <0.5 acre (too small) |
| **Soil quality** | Fertile (for kitchen garden) | Average | Contaminated/barren |
| **Internet** | 4G/fiber available | 4G with booster | No coverage |
| **Legal status** | Clear title, agricultural land | Conversion possible | Disputed ownership |

## Common Mistakes

- вқҢ Buying land before validating the business model
- вқҢ Over-building for future scale (build for current needs, plan for expansion)
- вқҢ Ignoring water and electricity availability
- вқҢ Not considering internet for content upload
- вқҢ Underestimating staffing costs
- вқҢ Forgetting about FSSAI requirements for the processing unit
