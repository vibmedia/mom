## PACKAGING STRATEGY OVERVIEW

### Glass vs Pouch Decision Matrix

| Factor | Glass Jar | Stand-up Pouch |
|--------|-----------|---------------|
| **Use for** | Pickles, Chutneys | Snacks, Meal kits, Quick-commerce |
| **Premium feel** | ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ | ⭐⭐⭐ |
| **Shipping cost** | High (heavy, fragile) | Low (light, flexible) |
| **Shelf life** | Excellent (non-reactive) | Good (with barrier layer) |
| **Gifting** | Perfect | Average |
| **Quick-commerce** | OK (breakage risk) | Perfect |
| **Cost per unit** | ₹15-20 | ₹5-8 |
| **Reusability** | Yes (brand touchpoint) | No |

### Label Must-Haves (FSSAI Compliance)
- Product name (Hindi + English)
- Net weight
- MRP + "Inclusive of all taxes"
- Ingredients list (descending order)
- Nutritional info (per 100g)
- FSSAI license number + logo
- Manufacturing date + Best before
- Manufacturer name + address
- "Mom Ka Nuska" brand logo + tagline
- Anjula ji's photo (trust element)
- "Zero Preservatives" badge
- QR code → farm video / product story

## DELHI WAREHOUSE & DISPATCH

### Why Delhi
- 60% of D2C orders ship to Delhi NCR / North India
- Quick-commerce dark stores are Delhi-centric
- B2B corporate gifting clients are mostly in Gurgaon/Noida
- Una → Delhi = ~350 km (6-7 hours by road, overnight logistics)

### Warehouse Strategy (Phased)

**Phase 1 (Month 1-6): Ship from Farm**
- All orders packed and shipped from Una farm
- Use Shiprocket/Delhivery for D2C
- Works for <500 orders/month
- Cost: ₹0 (no warehouse rent)

**Phase 2 (Month 7-12): Shared 3PL in Delhi**
- Partner with a 3PL like Shiprocket Fulfillment or WareIQ
- Shared warehouse in Gurgaon/Okhla
- Send bulk stock from Una → Delhi 3PL weekly
- 3PL handles pick-pack-ship for Delhi NCR orders
- Cost: ₹5-8 per order (fulfillment) + ₹3-5K/month (storage)
- Benefit: Same-day/next-day delivery in Delhi NCR

**Phase 3 (Year 2): Dedicated Space**
- 500-1000 sq ft dedicated area in shared warehouse
- Support quick-commerce inventory (Blinkit/Zepto dark store supply)
- Cost: ₹15-25K/month

### Recommended 3PL Partners

| Provider | Location | Best For | Est. Cost |
|----------|----------|----------|-----------|
| **Shiprocket Fulfillment** | Multiple (Delhi NCR) | Small D2C brands, easy Shopify integration | ₹5-8/order + ₹3K/month |
| **WareIQ** | Gurgaon, Noida | Tech-first, good for scaling | ₹6-10/order |
| **Pickrr Plus** | Delhi NCR | Budget-friendly for startups | ₹4-7/order |
| **Emiza** | Bhiwandi, Delhi | Multi-channel (Amazon+D2C) | ₹7-12/order |

### Logistics Flow

```
Farm (Una, HP)
    │
    ├──→ [Bulk shipment weekly] ──→ Delhi 3PL Warehouse
    │                                    │
    │                                    ├──→ D2C orders (Delhi NCR: same/next day)
    │                                    ├──→ B2B orders (corporate bulk)
    │                                    ├──→ Quick-commerce dark stores (Blinkit/Zepto)
    │                                    └──→ Amazon FBA warehouse transfer
    │
    └──→ [Direct ship for non-Delhi] ──→ Shiprocket/Delhivery (3-5 day delivery)
```

### Free Shipping Strategy
- Free shipping above ₹499 (pushes AOV up)
- Flat ₹49 for orders below ₹499
- Free shipping always for Delhi NCR (competitive advantage)
- B2B: shipping included in bulk pricing
