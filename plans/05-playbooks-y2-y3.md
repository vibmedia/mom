## PLAYBOOK 1: B2B CORPORATE GIFTING

### Why B2B is Critical
- **₹50L target** from gifting alone (50% of ₹1Cr Year 1 goal)
- Near-zero CAC (direct outreach, no ads needed)
- Bulk orders = production efficiency
- 35-40% margin (lower than B2C 60% but no ad spend)
- One corporate client = 50-200 gift boxes = ₹25K-1.6L per order
- **Proves revenue to Round 1 investors** — B2B is "real revenue" not just D2C hope

### Gift Box Products

| Box | Contents | Price | Margin |
|-----|----------|-------|--------|
| **Classic** (₹499) | 2 pickles (250g) + 1 papad pack | ₹499 | 40% |
| **Premium** (₹699) | 3 pickles (250g) + 1 papad + 1 masala | ₹699 | 38% |

| Segment | Examples | Qty/Order | Why |
|---------|----------|-----------|-----|
| IT/Tech (30) | TCS, Infosys, Wipro local offices, startups | 50-500 boxes | Large teams, good budgets, value "authentic" |
| Banks/Finance (15) | SBI, HDFC, ICICI branches | 30-100 | Client gifting culture |

### Outreach Timeline

**July (Month 3) — Research & Setup**
- Build 100-company prospect list with HR/Admin contacts
- Create B2B catalog (PDF + WhatsApp-friendly images)
- Design sample box for demos
- LinkedIn profile optimization for B2B outreach
- Prepare pricing sheet with bulk discounts (10%: 50+, 15%: 100+, 20%: 200+)

**August (Month 4) — Seed & Sample**
- Send 10 free sample boxes to top prospects
- Start LinkedIn messaging (10/day): "Diwali corporate gifting — homemade, preservative-free"
- Email campaign to HR mailing lists
- WhatsApp broadcast to business contacts
- Follow up on every sample within 48 hours
- Target: 5 confirmed orders by month-end

**September (Month 5) — BOOKING MONTH (Critical)**
- Intensify outreach: 20 calls/day
- Run "Early Bird 10% off" for September bookings
- Attend 2-3 local business networking events
- WhatsApp group for B2B clients (updates, new designs)
- Target: 15-20 confirmed orders (₹30-40L pipeline)
- Collect 50% advance on all orders

**October (Month 6) — PRODUCTION + SHIPPING**
- All B2B production on dedicated line (don't mix with B2C)
- Ship first batch by Oct 15 (pre-Diwali delivery)
- Quality check every box before dispatch
- Include thank-you card + "order again for New Year" offer
- Target: ₹15-20L shipped

**November (Month 7) — FINAL DELIVERY**
- All remaining orders delivered by Nov 5 (before Dhanteras Nov 6)
- Collect remaining payments
- Send thank-you + feedback request to every client
- Ask for referrals: "Know another company that might want these?"
- Target: ₹20-25L shipped, ₹50L total B2B season

**December-January — NEW YEAR + NEXT SEASON PREP**
- New Year gifting push (smaller wave)
- Collect testimonials for next year's pitch
- Start next season interest list

## PLAYBOOK 2: SHARK TANK INDIA APPLICATION

### Timeline
| When | Action |
|------|--------|
| **Jan 2027** | Applications typically open — monitor Sony LIV + social channels |
| **Jan W2** | Fill application form (company details, revenue, story) |
| **Jan W3** | Record 3-min pitch video (Vibhor + Anjula on farm) |
| **Feb** | Follow up, prepare for callback |
| **Mar-Apr** | If selected: mock pitches (5-10 rehearsals) |
| **May-Jul** | Typical filming window |
| **Oct-Dec** | Typical airing window |

### Pitch Structure (3 minutes)
1. **Hook (15s):** Anjula ji walks into frame on the farm, says "Main hoon Anjula, aur yeh hai Mom Ka Nuska"
2. **Problem (30s):** "Har Indian ko maa ke haath ka swaad chahiye, lekin factory ka khana mil raha hai"
3. **Solution (30s):** Show products, the farm, the process — "Asli maa, asli farm, asli swaad"
4. **Traction (30s):** Revenue numbers, order count, B2B clients, repeat rate, social following
5. **Ask (15s):** "₹50L for 5% equity" or appropriate amount based on growth
6. **Emotional close (30s):** "Mera beta technology samjhta hai, main swaad samjhti hoon. Saath mein hum India ko ghar ka swaad de rahe hain"

### What Sharks Will Ask
- Unit economics? → Have COGS, margin, CAC, LTV ready
- Why won't MTR/Mothers Recipe crush you? → Content moat + authentic story
- Can you scale without Anjula ji? → Train women like FarmDidi model (future)
- What's your repeat rate? → Target 20%+ by then
- Revenue projection? → Year 2 ₹5-8Cr with quick-commerce

### Even Without a Deal
Jhaji Store got NO deal in Season 1 but sales TRIPLED overnight. The TV exposure alone is worth ₹2-5Cr in equivalent ad spend.

## PLAYBOOK 3: QUICK-COMMERCE ONBOARDING

### Target Platforms
| Platform | When to Apply | Requirements |
|----------|--------------|--------------|
| **Blinkit** | Dec 2026 (Month 8) | FSSAI, ₹50L+ revenue, good packaging |
| **Zepto** | Dec 2026 | FSSAI, brand presence, shelf-ready |
| **BigBasket** | Jan 2027 | FSSAI, minimum 5 SKUs |
| **Swiggy Instamart** | Feb 2027 | FSSAI, metro presence |

### Onboarding Steps
1. **Apply via partner portal** (each platform has online onboarding)
2. **Submit:** FSSAI license, GST, product catalog, pricing, brand deck
3. **Negotiation:** Platform margin 25-35% (vs D2C 0%)
4. **Dark store listing:** Start with 3-5 hero SKUs in 2-3 cities
5. **Initial inventory:** Send stock to their warehouse (MOQ varies)
6. **Go live:** First 30 days = critical (run platform ads to boost ranking)
