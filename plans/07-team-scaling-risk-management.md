## PART 2: RISK MANAGEMENT — FOR THE TEAM

### Risk Register (Internal)

#### 1. PRODUCT RISKS

| Risk | Probability | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|------------|--------|------------|
| Inconsistent taste across batches | High | Critical | Document every recipe as SOP with exact measurements. Anjula ji approves every batch. Batch tracking register. |
| Shelf life shorter than expected | Medium | High | Lab test every product for 6-month shelf life BEFORE launching. Use glass jars (non-reactive). Include "Best before 6 months" conservatively. |
| Packaging breakage during shipping | Medium | Medium | Double-wrap glass jars. Use corrugated boxes with dividers. Test ship 20 packages before launch. Switch to pouch for quick-commerce. |
| Seasonal ingredient unavailability | Medium | High | Bulk buy during season (May-Jun for mangoes, Oct-Nov for amla). Build 60-day raw material buffer. Identify 2-3 backup suppliers per ingredient. |

#### 2. FARM RISKS (2-Acre Farm in Una)

| Risk | Probability | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|------------|--------|------------|
| Power outages disrupting production | High | Medium | Solar backup (₹1-2L investment). UPS for router/equipment. Schedule production during daylight hours. |
| Water shortage (Una summers) | Medium | High | Borewell + rainwater harvesting. Drip irrigation for farm crops. Build 10,000L water tank as buffer. |
| Wildlife crop damage (boar, monkeys) | High | Medium | Solar power fencing around farm perimeter (₹50K-1L). Mesh protection for drying area. |
| Land lease issues (Section 118) | Low | Critical | Get registered 10-15 year lease. Consult Shimla revenue lawyer. Keep CLU docs ready for processing unit. |
| Natural disaster (flooding, hail) | Low | High | Insure crops under PMFBY scheme. Keep covered drying area. Maintain 30-day finished goods buffer. |

#### 3. FINANCIAL RISKS

| Risk | Probability | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|------------|--------|------------|
| Burn rate exceeds revenue | Medium | Critical | Founders take ZERO salary Year 1. Monthly P&L review. Kill underperforming SKUs fast. Maintain 3-month cash runway always. |
| B2B clients don't pay on time | Medium | High | Collect 50% advance on all B2B orders. Net-15 payment terms max. No credit for first-time clients. |
| CAC stays above ₹150 | Medium | High | Double down on content (organic acquisition). A/B test 10+ ad creatives monthly. Focus on repeat rate (LTV > CAC). |
| Festive season underperformance | Low | Critical | Pre-book B2B orders in Sep with advances. Don't over-produce — make in batches. Have backup plan (liquidation via Amazon deals). |

#### 4. TEAM RISKS

| Risk | Probability | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|------------|--------|------------|
| Founder burnout (3 people, all roles) | High | Critical | Clear role boundaries from Day 1. Weekly 1-hour review (not daily chaos). Hire packing help EARLY (Month 3). Each person gets 1 day off/week minimum. |
| Anjula ji health/availability | Low | Critical | Document EVERY recipe as video SOP (not just written). Train 1 production helper as backup by Month 6. Her knowledge must be codified, not just in her head. |
| Srishti splits time with @stresss_tea | Medium | Medium | Clear MKN content schedule (1 reel/day, non-negotiable). @stresss_tea gets 2x/week cross-promotion (structured, not ad-hoc). |
| Key hire doesn't work out | Medium | Medium | 1-month trial period for all hires. Hire from Una/local area (lower cost, higher retention). Always have backup candidates identified. |

#### 5. OPERATIONAL RISKS

| Risk | Probability | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|------------|--------|------------|
| FSSAI approval delayed | Medium | High | Apply Day 1. Start with FSSAI registration (basic, instant for small units). Upgrade to license as revenue grows. Don't wait for license to start selling. |
| Shipping damages increase | Medium | Medium | Partner with reliable courier (Delhivery > others for fragile). Add ₹10/order packaging insurance. Monitor damage rate weekly (target <2%). |
| Delhi 3PL quality issues | Low | Medium | Visit 3PL monthly. Mystery-order yourself to check quality. Have backup 3PL identified. |

### Risk Response Playbook

| Scenario | Trigger | Action |
|----------|---------|--------|
| Revenue below 50% of plan for 2 months | <₹50K revenue by Month 3 | Pivot to pure B2B (corporate/events). Cut ad spend. Focus on organic content. |
| CAC above ₹200 for 30 days | High CAC, low conversions | Pause all paid ads. Go 100% organic for 30 days. Relaunch with new creative angle. |
| Stockout on bestseller | Hero product runs out | Same-day emergency production. Never let top 3 products go below 7-day stock. |
| Founder conflict | Disagreement on direction | Weekly 30-min founders sync. Pre-agree: Vibhor = business calls, Anjula = product calls, Srishti = brand calls. |
