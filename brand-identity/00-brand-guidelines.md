### Hindi (Primary — On all packaging and hero elements)

| Use | Font | Style |
|-----|------|-------|
| **Headlines** | Yatra One / Noto Sans Devanagari Bold | Warm, rounded, friendly |
| **Body** | Noto Sans Devanagari Regular | Clean, readable |

### English (Secondary)

| Use | Font | Style |
|-----|------|-------|
| **Headlines** | Playfair Display | Elegant serif, premium feel |
| **Sub-headings** | Outfit Semi-Bold | Modern, clean |
| **Body** | Outfit Regular | Readable, friendly |

### Rules
- Hindi text is ALWAYS larger and more prominent than English
- Product names appear in Hindi first, English below
- Brand name "मॉम का नुस्खा" uses the custom logotype — never type it in a system font
- Minimum font size on labels: 8pt for legal text, 12pt for product name

- ✅ Minimum size: 30mm width for print, 120px for digital
- ✅ Can be placed on Warm Cream, white, or dark brown backgrounds
- ❌ Never stretch, rotate, or alter proportions

- ❌ Never change the logo colors outside the approved palette

## PHOTOGRAPHY STYLE

### Do's
- Warm, natural lighting (golden hour or window light)
- Rustic surfaces: wooden boards, clay plates, brass utensils
- Real hands (Anjula ji's hands) in product-making shots
- Scattered raw ingredients around the product
- Farm backdrop (green fields, sunlight, earthen walls)
- "Messy-authentic" — not sterile studio shots

### Don'ts
- No white/sterile studio backgrounds
- No stock photography
- No perfectly arranged flat-lays (keep it slightly imperfect = authentic)
- No modern kitchen settings
- No plastic utensils or synthetic surfaces in frame

### Shot Types for Each SKU
1. **Hero shot:** Product on rustic surface, warm light, ingredients scattered
2. **Process shot:** Hands making the product (mixing, packing, sealing)
3. **Lifestyle shot:** Product on a dining table with roti/rice (in-use context)
4. **Ingredient shot:** Close-up of raw ingredients (fresh mangoes, green chillies)
5. **Farm shot:** Product held up with farm in background

## VOICE & TONE

### Brand Voice

| Attribute | Description | Example |
|-----------|------------|---------|
| **Warm** | Like talking to your own mother | "Aaj aam ka achar daala — ghar mein mehak aa gayi" |
| **Honest** | No marketing gimmicks | "Haan, humara pickle thoda mehenga hai. Kyunki asli ghee aur kacchi ghani ka tel daalte hain." |
| **Proud** | Not modest — proud of tradition | "30 saal purana nuskha hai — koi factory copy nahi kar sakti" |
| **Simple** | No corporate jargon | "Farm se seedha aapke ghar" not "Vertically integrated farm-to-fork supply chain" |

### Do Say
- "Maa ke haath ka swaad"
- "Ghar jaisa, kyunki ghar pe bana hai"
- "Zero preservatives, bas maa ka pyaar"
- "Humari farm, humara achar, aapka bharosa"

### Don't Say
- "Artisanal" (too Western/pretentious)
- "Curated" (too corporate)
- "Disrupting the pickle industry" (cringe startup speak)
- "Premium" as the main selling point (quality speaks for itself)

### Language Mix
- Instagram captions: 70% Hindi, 30% English (Hinglish is fine)
- Product labels: Hindi primary + English secondary
- Website: Bilingual (Hindi hero text + English body for SEO)
- B2B emails: English (professional context)
