## SLIDE 7: DELHI WAREHOUSE & LOGISTICS

### Prompt
```
Design a 16:9 slide titled "Logistics — Farm to Kitchen". Show a flow diagram: Farm (Una, HP) → Delhi 3PL Warehouse → 4 delivery channels (D2C same-day, B2B bulk, Quick-commerce dark stores, Amazon FBA). Include phased warehouse strategy below. Cream bg, terracotta flow arrows.
```

### Data

**Why Delhi:**
- 60% of D2C orders ship to Delhi NCR / North India
- Quick-commerce dark stores are Delhi-centric
- B2B corporate clients mostly in Gurgaon/Noida
- Una → Delhi = ~350 km (6-7 hours road, overnight logistics)

**Phased Strategy:**

| Phase | When | What | Cost |
|-------|------|------|------|
| Phase 1 (Month 1-6) | Ship from Farm | All orders packed at Una, Shiprocket/Delhivery for D2C. Works for <500 orders/month | ₹0 warehouse rent |
| Phase 2 (Month 7-12) | Shared 3PL Delhi | Partner with Shiprocket Fulfillment / WareIQ. Bulk stock Una → Delhi weekly. 3PL handles pick-pack-ship for NCR | ₹5-8/order + ₹3-5K/month storage |
| Phase 3 (Year 2) | Dedicated Space | 500-1000 sqft in shared warehouse. Supports quick-commerce inventory | ₹15-25K/month |

**Logistics Flow:**
```
Farm (Una, HP)
    │
    ├──→ [Bulk shipment weekly] ──→ Delhi 3PL Warehouse
    │                                    │
    │                                    ├──→ D2C orders (Delhi NCR: same/next day)
    │                                    ├──→ B2B orders (corporate bulk)
    │                                    ├──→ Quick-commerce dark stores
    │                                    └──→ Amazon FBA warehouse transfer
    │
    └──→ [Direct ship for non-Delhi] ──→ Shiprocket/Delhivery (3-5 days)
```

**3PL Partners:**
| Provider | Location | Best For | Cost |
|----------|----------|----------|------|
| Shiprocket Fulfillment | Delhi NCR | Small D2C, Shopify integration | ₹5-8/order |
| WareIQ | Gurgaon, Noida | Tech-first, scaling | ₹6-10/order |
| Pickrr Plus | Delhi NCR | Budget startups | ₹4-7/order |
| Emiza | Bhiwandi, Delhi | Multi-channel | ₹7-12/order |

**Shipping Costs:**
| Zone | 500g | Delivery |
|------|------|----------|
| Delhi NCR (from 3PL) | ₹35-45 | Same/Next day |
| North India | ₹55-70 | 2-3 days |
| Rest of India | ₹70-90 | 3-5 days |
| NRI (UAE/US) | ₹400-600 | 7-10 days |

**Free Shipping:** Above ₹499 | Flat ₹49 below | Always free for Delhi NCR

## SLIDE 8: GLASS vs POUCH DECISION

### Prompt
```
Design a 16:9 slide titled "Packaging Strategy — Glass vs Pouch". Show a comparison table with 2 columns: glass jar and stand-up pouch, with icons for each factor. Include recommendation for when to use each. Cream bg.
```

### Data

| Factor | Glass Jar | Stand-up Pouch |
|--------|-----------|---------------|
| Use for | Pickles, Chutneys | Snacks, Meal kits, Quick-commerce |
| Premium feel | ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ | ⭐⭐⭐ |
| Shipping cost | High (heavy, fragile) | Low (light, flexible) |
| Shelf life | Excellent (non-reactive) | Good (with barrier layer) |
| Gifting | Perfect | Average |
| Quick-commerce | OK (breakage risk) | Perfect |
| Cost per unit | ₹15-20 | ₹5-8 |
| Reusability | Yes (brand touchpoint) | No |
| Instagrammable | Very (glass catches light) | Moderate |

**Decision:** Use glass for premium products (pickles, chutneys, gifts) and pouches for volume products (snacks, quick-commerce, trials)
