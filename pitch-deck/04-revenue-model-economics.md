## SLIDE 1: REVENUE MODEL OVERVIEW

### Prompt
```
Design a 16:9 slide titled "Revenue Model — 4 Channels". Show 4 revenue channels as vertical bars/pillars with icons and % contribution. Left to right: D2C Website, B2B Corporate Gifting, Marketplaces (Amazon/Flipkart), Quick-Commerce. Show Year 1 vs Year 3 split below. Cream bg, terracotta bars.
```

### Data

**4 Revenue Channels:**

| Channel | Year 1 (₹1Cr) | Year 3 (₹50Cr) | How |
|---------|---------------|----------------|-----|
| D2C Website | 30% = ₹30L | 25% = ₹12.5Cr | Shopify/WooCommerce → Instagram → Direct orders |
| B2B Corporate Gifting | 50% = ₹50L | 15% = ₹7.5Cr | Festival gift boxes, bulk orders, Diwali/Christmas |
| Marketplaces (Amazon/Flipkart) | 15% = ₹15L | 25% = ₹12.5Cr | Discovery channel, Amazon ads, organic ranking |
| Quick-Commerce (Blinkit/Zepto) | 5% = ₹5L | 35% = ₹17.5Cr | Dark store listing, impulse buys, urban metros |

**Revenue Growth Path:**
- Year 1: ₹1 Crore (proving the model)
- Year 2: ₹5-8 Crore (scaling channels)
- Year 3: ₹25-50 Crore (quick-commerce + B2B explosion)

**Why B2B is 50% in Year 1:**
- Corporate gifting is HIGH AOV (₹500-₹1500 per box)
- Diwali alone = ₹25-30L if 300 boxes × ₹800 avg
- Predictable bulk orders with 50% advance payment
- Less marketing spend needed (direct outreach)
- After Year 1: D2C + Quick-commerce overtake B2B

## SLIDE 2: D2C UNIT ECONOMICS

### Prompt
```
Design a 16:9 slide titled "D2C Unit Economics". Show a waterfall chart flowing from Revenue per Order → COGS → Gross Profit → Ad Spend → Shipping → Net Contribution. Each step as a bar dropping down. Right side: key metrics in a card. Cream bg, green for profit bars, red for cost bars.
```

### Data

**D2C Economics (Per Order):**

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Average Order Value (AOV) | ₹500 |
| Products per order | 2-3 jars |
| COGS (avg 27%) | ₹135 |
| **Gross Profit** | **₹365 (73%)** |
| Shipping cost (₹499+ = free) | ₹60 (company bears) |
| Packaging (outer box, bubble wrap) | ₹25 |
| Payment gateway (2%) | ₹10 |
| **Contribution Margin** | **₹270 (54%)** |
| CAC (blended) | ₹100-150 |
| **Net Contribution per Order** | **₹120-170** |
| Repeat rate (target Year 1) | 25% |
| LTV (3 orders avg) | ₹1,500 |
| LTV:CAC ratio | 10-15x |

**Monthly D2C Math (Month 6 target):**
| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Orders/month | 300 |
| AOV | ₹500 |
| Revenue | ₹1.5L |
| Ad spend | ₹30K |
| COGS | ₹40K |
| Shipping | ₹18K |
| Net contribution | ₹62K |

**CAC Trajectory:**
| Month | CAC | Why |
|-------|-----|-----|
| Month 1-3 | ₹150 | Cold ads, no brand awareness |
| Month 4-6 | ₹120 | Content starts working, retargeting |
| Month 7-9 | ₹100 | Organic 30%+, lower paid dependency |
| Month 10-12 | ₹80 | Repeat orders, referrals, word of mouth |

**Warm Audience Advantage (from @pahadansteam/@stresss_tea research):**
- Warm audience conversation cost: ₹15-35 per chat (vs ₹150+ cold ad)
- 6 months of pre-launch content builds warm audience before first sale
- Day 1 buyers are already "sold" — they've watched 100+ Reels

6. Delivery 1 week before festival
7. Post-delivery: thank you + next festival pitch
