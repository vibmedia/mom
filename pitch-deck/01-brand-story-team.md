## SLIDE 2: THE PROBLEM

### Prompt
```
Design a 16:9 slide titled "The Problem" with cream background. Show 3 problem blocks vertically stacked on left with hand-drawn icons. Right side: a faded photo of a factory assembly line making pickles (contrast to homemade). Bottom: bold statement in terracotta. Playfair Display headings, Outfit body.
```

### Data
**Problem 1 — The Market is Broken**
- ₹25,000+ Crore Indian pickle/papad/chutney market
- Growing 8-10% YoY
- Dominated by factory brands: MTR, Mothers Recipe, Priya, Tops, Patanjali
- All use preservatives, artificial colors, mass production
- "Ghar jaisa swaad" is just a marketing claim, not reality

**Problem 2 — Urban Indians Miss Mom's Taste**
- 50M+ Indians living away from home in metros
- No trusted source for authentic homemade food
- WhatsApp groups sell unbranded, unregulated pickles
- No hygiene standards, no content, no brand trust

**Problem 3 — The Trust Gap**
- Big brands = preservatives + factory made (consumers know this)
- Small sellers = no FSSAI, no traceability, inconsistent quality
- NO brand combines: authentic homemade + food safety + digital-first + content storytelling

**The Gap:** A TRUSTED, content-driven homemade food brand with a real mother, a real farm, and real recipes — sold D2C with zero preservatives.

### Speaker Notes
- "Maa ke haath ka achar sabko chahiye — par koi bharosa ke saath nahi de raha"
- Emphasize: we're not competing with MTR (mass market), we're creating a NEW category

## SLIDE 7: PACKAGING SYSTEM

### Prompt
```
Design a 16:9 slide titled "Packaging — Premium Rustic" showing 4 packaging formats. Each format as a card with photo + specs. Left to right: Glass jar (pickles), Stand-up pouch (snacks), Gift box (festive), Pre-cook kit (meal kits). Below: label anatomy diagram showing all elements. Cream bg, terracotta accents.
```

### Data

**Format 1 — Glass Jar (Pickles & Chutneys)**
- Jar: Hexagonal glass, 200ml (250g) / 400ml (500g)
- Lid: Gold twist-off metal, 63mm diameter
- Label: 100mm x 70mm, kraft paper, matte lamination, waterproof
- Print: 4-color offset
- Seal: Clear shrink band with brand print
- Cost: ₹12-15 per 200ml jar, ₹18-22 per 400ml jar
- Feels: Premium, reusable, instagrammable

**Format 2 — Stand-up Pouch (Snacks)**
- Material: Kraft paper front + metalised BOPP back (moisture barrier)
- Size: 200g (18cm x 28cm) / 400g (22cm x 32cm)
- Window: Clear oval (6cm x 4cm) to show product
- Closure: Zip-lock resealable
- Print: 6-color rotogravure
- Cost: ₹5-8 per pouch
- Feels: Modern, convenient, lighter shipping

**Format 3 — Festive Gift Box**
- Material: 3-ply corrugated + kraft paper lamination
- Size: 32cm x 25cm x 10cm (fits 4 x 250g jars)
- Handle: Jute rope (handmade feel)
- Window: Clear PET to show jars
- Branding: Hot-stamped gold foil for "मॉम का नुस्खा"
- Interior: Jute cloth dividers
- Insert: Thank-you card + QR code to farm video
- Tiers: Classic ₹499 | Premium ₹699 | Royal ₹999 | Grand ₹1,499
- Cost: ₹45-65 per box

**Format 4 — Pre-cook Kit**
- Masala: Flat pouch with zip-lock (100-150g)
- Sleeve: Cardboard wrap with recipe card + QR to video
- Light, flat, cheap to ship

**Label Must-Haves (FSSAI):**
- Product name (Hindi + English)
- Net weight + MRP inclusive of taxes
- Ingredients list (descending order)
- Nutritional info per 100g
- FSSAI license number + logo
- Manufacturing date + Best before
- Manufacturer name + address
- Brand logo + "Zero Preservatives" badge
- Anjula ji's photo (trust element)
- QR code → farm video / product story
