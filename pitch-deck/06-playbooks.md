## SLIDE 4: QUICK-COMMERCE PLAYBOOK

### Prompt
```
Design a 16:9 slide titled "Playbook: Quick-Commerce (Blinkit/Zepto/Instamart)". Show listing process, dark store strategy, and revenue projections. Include logos of Blinkit, Zepto, Swiggy Instamart. Cream bg.
```

### Data

**Quick-Commerce Onboarding Process:**

| Step | When | What |
|------|------|------|
| 1. Research | Month 7 | Understand each platform's onboarding requirements |
| 2. Seller registration | Month 8 | Apply on Blinkit seller portal (primary target) |
| 3. Category approval | Month 8-9 | Submit: FSSAI, product images, pricing, MOQ |
| 4. Listing live | Month 9 | 4-6 hero SKUs listed (best sellers only) |
| 5. Dark store stocking | Month 9 | Ship inventory to dark stores (Delhi NCR first) |
| 6. Optimize | Month 10+ | Monitor: search ranking, reviews, conversion |

**Platform Strategy:**
| Platform | Priority | Why | Commission |
|----------|----------|-----|-----------|
| Blinkit | #1 | Largest in Delhi NCR, Zomato integration | 15-20% |
| Zepto | #2 | Fast growing, good for impulse buys | 15-18% |
| Swiggy Instamart | #3 | Good reach, Swiggy brand trust | 18-22% |
| BigBasket | #4 | More planned purchases, less impulse | 20-25% |

**Quick-Commerce Pricing Strategy:**
- MRP = D2C price (₹229 for 250g pickle)
- Platform takes 15-20% commission
- Net realization: ₹183-195 per unit
- Still 65-70% margin after commission
- Volume makes up for lower per-unit margin

**Revenue Projections (Quick-Commerce):**
| Month | Orders/Month | Revenue |
|-------|-------------|---------|
| Month 9 (launch) | 100 | ₹50K |
| Month 10 | 200 | ₹1L |
| Month 11 | 400 | ₹2L |
| Month 12 | 600 | ₹3L |
| Year 2 Q1 | 2,000/month | ₹10L/month |
| Year 2 Q4 | 5,000/month | ₹25L/month |
| Year 3 | 15,000/month | ₹75L/month |

**Dark Store Inventory Management:**
- Stock 7-day supply per dark store
- Start with 10 dark stores (Delhi NCR)
- Expand to 50+ dark stores (NCR + Mumbai + Bangalore) by Year 2
- Replenishment: 3x/week from Delhi 3PL warehouse
- SKU selection for QC: Top 6 bestsellers only (not full range)
