## SLIDE 3: PRODUCT RISK REGISTER

### Prompt
```
Design a 16:9 slide titled "Risk Register — Product & Operations". Show a risk matrix with probability vs impact. Plot 8-10 risks. Color code: green (manageable), yellow (watch), red (critical). Cream bg.
```

### Data

**Product Risks:**
| Risk | Probability | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|------------|--------|------------|
| Inconsistent taste across batches | High | Critical | SOP every recipe with exact measurements. Anjula ji approves every batch. Batch tracking register. |
| Shelf life shorter than expected | Medium | High | Lab test every product for 6-month life BEFORE launch. Glass jars (non-reactive). "Best before 6 months" conservative. |
| Packaging breakage in shipping | Medium | Medium | Double-wrap glass. Corrugated boxes with dividers. Test-ship 20 packages first. Pouch for quick-commerce. |
| Seasonal ingredient unavailability | Medium | High | Bulk buy during season (May-Jun mangoes, Oct-Nov amla). 60-day raw material buffer. 2-3 backup suppliers per ingredient. |

**Farm Risks:**
| Risk | Probability | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|------------|--------|------------|
| Power outages | High | Medium | Solar backup (₹1-2L). UPS for router. Daylight production schedule. |
| Water shortage (summers) | Medium | High | Borewell + rainwater harvesting. Drip irrigation. 10,000L water tank buffer. |
| Wildlife crop damage | High | Medium | Solar power fencing (₹50K-1L). Mesh for drying area. |
| Land lease issues (Section 118) | Low | Critical | Registered 10-15 year lease. Shimla revenue lawyer. CLU docs ready. |
| Natural disaster (flood/hail) | Low | High | PMFBY crop insurance. Covered drying area. 30-day finished goods buffer. |
