> Generated using `fmcg-farm-setup` skill | April 2026

## Executive Summary

Build a **triple-duty farm** (1-2 acres) that serves as: (1) food production kitchen, (2) content creation studio, and (3) ingredient testing garden. **Estimated setup cost: ₹10-20L.** Recommend leasing first for 12-24 months before buying.

---

## 1. Land Selection Criteria

| Factor | Must Have | Nice to Have |
|--------|----------|-------------|
| **Distance from city** | 30-60 km from base city | Under 45 km |
| **Road access** | Paved road to gate | Highway-adjacent |
| **Water** | Borewell OR municipal supply | Both + rainwater harvesting |
| **Electricity** | Single phase minimum | 3-phase power |
| **Internet** | 4G coverage | Fiber available |
| **Land size** | 0.5-1 acre (start) | 1-2 acres (growth) |
| **Legal** | Clear title, non-disputed | Agricultural classification |
| **Soil** | Functional for kitchen garden | Fertile, tested |
| **Security** | Fencing possible | Watchman/neighbor presence |
