## 1. Incorporation — Pvt Ltd (Recommended)

### Why Pvt Ltd?

| Factor | Pvt Ltd ✅ | LLP | Sole Prop |
|--------|----------|-----|-----------|
| Raise equity (angel/VC) | ✅ Best | ❌ Very hard | ❌ Impossible |
| Credibility with banks | High | Medium | Low |
| Limited liability | ✅ | ✅ | ❌ Personal risk |
| Compliance burden | High (annual audit) | Medium | Low |
| Cost | ₹8-15K | ₹5-10K | ₹1-3K |

### Incorporation Checklist

| # | Step | Timeline | Cost | Status |
|---|------|----------|------|--------|
| 1 | Obtain DSC (Digital Signature Certificate) for 2 directors | 1-2 days | ₹1,000-1,500 | [ ] |
| 2 | Apply DIN (Director Identification Number) | 1-2 days | Included | [ ] |
| 3 | Reserve company name via RUN/SPICe+ Part A | 1-3 days | ₹1,000 | [ ] |
| 4 | File SPICe+ (PAN + TAN + GST in one shot) | 3-7 days | ₹3,000-5,000 | [ ] |
| 5 | Receive CoI (Certificate of Incorporation) | — | — | [ ] |
| 6 | Open Current Account (HDFC/ICICI/Kotak recommended) | 1-7 days | Free | [ ] |
| 7 | Issue share certificates | Same day | Internal | [ ] |
| 8 | File INC-20A (commencement of business) | Within 180 days | ₹500 | [ ] |

## 2. FSSAI License

### Phase-wise Licensing

| Phase | License | When | Turnover | Fee | Timeline |

### Monthly GST Compliance

| Task | Due Date | Who |
|------|----------|-----|
| GSTR-3B filing | 20th of next month | CA |
| GSTR-1 (outward supplies) | 11th or quarterly (if under ₹5Cr) | CA |
| Payment of GST liability | With GSTR-3B | CA |
| Annual return GSTR-9 | Dec 31 of next FY | CA |

> **Critical:** File GSTR-3B from Month 1, even if returns are NIL. This builds GST compliance history which banks need for loans.

## 4. Trademark Protection

### Filing Strategy

| Trademark | Class | Covers | Est. Cost |
|-----------|-------|--------|-----------|
| "Mom Ka Nuska" (wordmark) | **Class 29** | Preserved fruits/vegetables, pickles, papad, namkeen | ₹4,500 (startup rate) |
| "Mom Ka Nuska" (wordmark) | **Class 30** | Spices, masala, condiments, sauces, chutney | ₹4,500 |
| Logo/device mark | **Class 29 + 30** | Visual brand identity | ₹9,000 |
| **Total** | | | **₹18,000** |

### Timeline

| Step | Duration |
|------|----------|
| File application | Day 0 |
| Vienna codification (if logo) | 1-2 months |
| Examination | 2-4 months |
| Published in TM Journal | Month 4-6 |
| Opposition period | 4 months |
| Registration (if no opposition) | Month 8-12 |
| ™ → ® | After registration |

> **Tip:** You can use ™ symbol immediately after filing. Use ® only after registration.

### Name Search Before Filing

1. Check on [ipindia.gov.in](https://ipindiaonline.gov.in/tmrpublicsearch/)
2. Search for: "Mom Ka Nuska", "Moms Nuska", "Mom Nuska", "Nuska" 
3. If taken, consider alternatives before finalizing branding

## 7. Legal Milestone Timeline

```
MONTH 0-1 ───────────────────────────────────
  ├── Incorporate Pvt Ltd (SPICe+)
  ├── Open current bank account  
  ├── File trademark (Class 29 + 30)
  ├── MSME/Udyam registration
  └── Hire CA, set up accounting

MONTH 1-2 ───────────────────────────────────
  ├── GST registration
  ├── FSSAI Basic Registration
  └── Founders' Agreement (SHA)

MONTH 3-6 ───────────────────────────────────
  ├── Professional liability insurance
  ├── Start filing GST returns (even NIL)
  └── Angel round → SAFE or SHA update

MONTH 6-12 ──────────────────────────────────
  ├── FSSAI State License (when turnover > ₹12L)
  ├── Shop & Establishment (for farm unit)
  └── Employee compliance (PF/ESI if hiring)

YEAR 1 ──────────────────────────────────────
  ├── First statutory audit
  ├── ROC annual filing (MCA)
  ├── FSSAI renewal
  └── Income tax return

YEAR 2-3 ────────────────────────────────────
  ├── ISO 22000 (food safety) — optional but adds trust
  ├── IEC registration (if NRI exports)
  └── APEDA registration (food exports)

YEAR 3-5 ────────────────────────────────────
  ├── Consider FSSAI Central License
  ├── FDA registration (if USA market)
  └── Country-specific labeling compliance
```
