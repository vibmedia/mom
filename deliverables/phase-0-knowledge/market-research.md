> **For** urban Indians who miss the taste of home-cooked food, **Mom Ka Nuska** is the **farm-to-kitchen food brand** that delivers **authentic, preservative-free products made by a real mother on a real farm** — unlike factory-made packaged food that compromises on taste and purity.
